About

SISSY'25 focuses on the theme of self-improving systems integration. It brings together researchers and practitioners working on systems that can adapt, learn, and evolve over time to meet complex integration challenges. This 12th edition of the workshop continues a tradition of exploring cutting-edge approaches in system autonomy, interoperability, and adaptive behavior.
